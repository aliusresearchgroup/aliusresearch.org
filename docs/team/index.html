In Memoriam

Martin Fortier

Co-Founder of ALIUS (2016) · 1990–2020

On April 11th, 2020, Martin Fortier — Co-Founder of ALIUS in 2016 — passed away after a long battle with cancer. He was thirty years old. ALIUS continues to honour his contributions to the scientific study of consciousness and the cultural anthropology of hallucinogenic experience.
