Bulletin Writing Instructions | Guidelines

ALIUS warmly welcomes members and external contributors to submit short pieces for publication in the rolling issues of the ALIUS Bulletin. Contributions may take the form of a written interview, an edited transcript of a recorded audio interview, a short podcast episode, a scientific commentary on popular-science depictions of altered states of consciousness, or a review of a relevant popular-science book. Please consult the guidelines below before preparing a submission; for interview pieces, the interview generator can help assemble the required materials.
